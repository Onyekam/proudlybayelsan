Bayelsa Young Entrepreneur's Network

For the purpose of providing encouragement and support (both financial and technical) to the young entrepreneurs of the state, his Excellency Governor Henry Seriake Dickson, directed the creation of a formal group, called the Bayelsa Youth Entrepreneurship Network. BYEN was inaugurated on Tuesday 12th June 2018, at the Exco Chambers, Bayelsa State Government House, with participants charged to focus on businesses and alliances that would grow the economy of the state.


The network covers a wide cross section of industries including:
